About Dalgona Coffee

Dalgona coffe is South Korean coffe dish, which went viralo during Lockdown.The dish can be made from a few recipes which are ready available in your kitchen cupboard. What are you waiting for?

Ingredients

  • Instant coffee powder or Expresso Granules
  • Hot Water
  • Sugar
  • Milk

Steps For Preparing Dalgona Coffee

Step 1: Add coffee powder and hot water
Add 2 tabelspoons of coffee powder and a cup of how water. Add 2 tabelspoons of sugar to the mixture.
Step 2: Stir the mixture well
Use a handheld mixture and stir the mixture well, till it turns creamy and fluffy.
Step 3: Serve it with Milk
Add a cup of hot milk and add the flufy mxture to milk.Serve it with a spoon and a straw. Now stir,stir and stir to enjoy the dish.
